About us

Who we are

We are a “dream team” ready to show you the largest country in Europe — Ukraine. We provide all-inclusive vacations, charters, tours and vacation packages for individuals, families and groups, and we'd love to do the same for you!

Why you should visit Ukraine

Have you ever seen amazing mountains, sea, forests, rivers, ancient culture, modern urban landscapes and the most beautiful girls in the world - all in one country?

Ukraine used to attract more than 20 million foreign citizens every year and which is not surprising. Ukraine is situated on the crossroads between central and Eastern Europe, between north and south. Amazing country!

Ukraine has vineyards where we produce wine, ruins of ancient castles, historical parks, many sites and temples of different world religions.

Kyiv, the capital, has many unique monuments such as Saint Sophia Cathedral and broad boulevards. There are other cities well-known to tourists such as Odesa located at the seaside and the old city of Lviv in the west. The Carpathian Mountains are suitable for skiing, hiking, fishing and hunting. The coastline of the Black Sea is a popular summer destination…

You will fall in LOVE with this country! We can organize absolutely unforgettable vacation for you!
